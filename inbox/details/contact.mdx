---
title: 'Contact Info'
description: 'Manage and keep lead information up to date.'
---

To add or edit contact info, navigate to the conversation with the lead (either through the Inbox, or by clicking on a lead in your CRM or Lists).

<video autoplay="true" muted="true" loop playsinline className="w-full aspect-video" src="/videos/guides_edit_contact.mp4" />
In the sidebar, simply fill in or edit the necessary contact details such as name, email, or website directly into the designated fields. Contact details will auto-save as you make changes, so you can move extremely quickly.
