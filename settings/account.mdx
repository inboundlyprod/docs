To manage your account details, go to the Settings tab and select "Profile" from the sidebar.

<video autoplay="true" muted="true" loop playsinline className="w-full aspect-video" src="/videos/guides_update_username.mp4" />

From here, you can:
* Update your username.
* Update your email address.
* Update your profile picture.
