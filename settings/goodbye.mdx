To delete your Inboundly account, go to the Settings tab and select "Security" from the sidebar. From there, click "Delete Account" and follow the instructions.

<video autoplay="true" muted="true" loop playsinline className="w-full aspect-video" src="/videos/guides_opensecurity.mp4" />

<Warning>If you delete your account, you will permanently delete all the data associated with your account.</Warning>

Deleting your account does not affect your billing plan. If you wish to cancel Inboundly completely, unsubscribe before deleting your account.
