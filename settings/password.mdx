---
title: 'Update Password'
description: 'Update your password in just 2 simple steps.'
---

To update your password, go to the Settings tab and select "Security" from the sidebar. From there, click "Update Password" and follow the instructions.

You'll receive a password reset email to the email you use to log in to Inboundly.
