title: 'Manage Billing'
description: 'Understand each team role to help you manage access and responsibilities within your workspace.'
---

<Note>Only workspace owners have access to manage billing details for their workspace.</Note>

To access billing, go to the Settings tab and select "Billing" from the sidebar.

To edit billing details, click "Manage". From here, you can view current subscription details, update payment information, upgrade plans and manage other related settings.

To upgrade your plan, choose from the available upgrades. From here, you can easily move to a more powerful plan.
