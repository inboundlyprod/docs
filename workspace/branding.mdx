---
title: 'Custom Branding'
description: 'Truly make your workspace yours with complete control over its look & feel.'
---

To add custom branding to your workspace, go to the Settings tab and select "Branding" from the sidebar.

From here, you can:
* Update the workspace **logo**, visible in the top-left corner of your dashboard.
* Update the workspace **tab icon**, which appears in your browser tab when Inboundly is open.
