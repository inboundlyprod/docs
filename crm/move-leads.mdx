Moving leads between labels is a simple process that can be done in many places on Inboundly.

In the CRM, you can drag and drop leads to quickly move them between labels.

<video autoplay="true" muted="true" loop playsinline className="w-full aspect-video" src="/videos/guides_drag_drop_leads.mp4" />

In your Inbox, CRM, and Lists, you can click on conversations/leads and use the right sidebar to change their label.
